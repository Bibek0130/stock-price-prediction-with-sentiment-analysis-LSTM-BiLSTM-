Why use LSTM-BiLSTM for Stock Price Prediction with Sentiment Analysis?

LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) and BiLSTM (Bidirectional LSTM) are popular deep learning architectures for sequential data, making them ideal for stock price prediction. Stock prices depend on time-series data, and LSTMs effectively capture long-term dependencies. The BiLSTM variant enhances this by processing data in both forward and backward directions, giving it a more comprehensive understanding of past and future trends. By combining LSTM-BiLSTM with sentiment analysis, which interprets market sentiment from news articles or social media, the model can account for both historical price data and emotional factors, improving prediction accuracy.
